## What is 1tap.video?

1tap.video is a revolutionary video recording platform that enables users to capture, edit, and share videos with just one tap. Built for modern workflows, it streamlines the entire video creation process from recording to distribution.

## How It Works

1. **Record** - Tap to start recording your screen or camera
2. **Edit** - AI automatically enhances your video with smart cuts and effects
3. **Share** - Get an instant shareable link for your video
4. **Track** - Monitor views and engagement with built-in analytics
