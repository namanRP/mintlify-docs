## Welcome to 1tap.video

Get up and running with 1tap.video in just a few minutes. This guide will walk you through creating your account, making your first recording, and sharing it with the world.

## Step 1: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Visit [1tap.video](https://1tap.video) and click "Get Started" to create your free account.
    
    You can sign up using:
    - Email and password
    - Google account
    - Microsoft account
    - Apple ID
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Your Email">
    Check your email inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Your Profile">
    Add your name, profile picture, and basic information to personalize your experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Make Your First Recording

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Recording Type">
    Select what you want to record:
    
    - **Screen**: Record your desktop or specific windows
    - **Camera**: Record using your device's camera
    - **Both**: Record screen and camera simultaneously
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Adjust your recording settings:
    
    - Resolution (HD, 4K)
    - Frame rate (30fps, 60fps)
    - Audio source (microphone, system audio)
    - Recording area (full screen, window, custom)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Recording">
    Click the record button or press the hotkey (Ctrl+Shift+R) to start recording.
    
    <Tip>
      You can pause and resume recording at any time using the pause button or hotkey.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Stop Recording">
    Click stop or press the hotkey again to end your recording.
    
    Your video will automatically be processed and uploaded to your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Edit Your Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Your Recording">
    Watch your video and identify areas that need editing or improvement.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use AI Editing">
    Let our AI automatically enhance your video:
    
    - Remove silent moments
    - Adjust brightness and contrast
    - Add smooth transitions
    - Suggest background music
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manual Adjustments">
    Make custom edits using our editing tools:
    
    - Trim video length
    - Add text overlays
    - Insert graphics or logos
    - Adjust audio levels
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 5: Share Your Video

<Steps>
  <Step title="Generate Share Link">
    Click "Share" to get a custom link for your video.
    
    You can customize:
    - Link expiration date
    - Password protection
    - Download permissions
    - View count limits
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Sharing Method">
    Share your video through:
    
    - Direct link sharing
    - Social media platforms
    - Email embedding
    - Team collaboration tools
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Performance">
    Monitor your video's performance with built-in analytics:
    
    - View count and engagement
    - Geographic distribution
    - Device and browser data
    - Watch time analytics
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Recording Issues">
    **Problem**: Recording doesn't start
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Check microphone and camera permissions
    - Ensure sufficient disk space
    - Restart the application
    - Update to the latest version
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Audio Problems">
    **Problem**: No audio in recording
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Check audio input settings
    - Test microphone in system settings
    - Ensure correct audio source is selected
    - Check volume levels
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Upload Failures">
    **Problem**: Video won't upload
    
    **Solutions**:
    - Check internet connection
    - Verify file size limits
    - Try uploading in smaller chunks
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
